About FlintyCo

Established in 2026, FlintyCo is a luxury jewelry brand. Our goal is to make unique and minimal pieces that will compliment any style. We have a huge team of wonderful and creative people, working hard to make the vision come true.

About the Developer

I am Fatema Ishraq,and I am the person who built this website. I have always been drawn to the jewelry sector of retail. For a luxury jewelry brand that specializes on selling expensive and minimal looking items, a clean and simple layout is going to best suit the website. Building FlintyCo was a way for me to show how I can take a high-end brand's 'look' and turn it into a website that is easy to use and looks sharp on any screen.
